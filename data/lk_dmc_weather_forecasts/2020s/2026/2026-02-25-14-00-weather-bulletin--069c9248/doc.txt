Bulletin No: 04 TCA/26/02/25/01 COLOR: Green

Weather Bulletin: Low-Pressure Area – Central Bay of Bengal Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre, Department of Meteorology. Issued at 2.00 p.m. on 25 January 2026

For fishermen and naval communities engaged in marine activities in

the Central Bay of Bengal, northeast of Sri Lanka.

The low-pressure area that prevailed over the central Bay of Bengal to the northeast of Sri Lanka has now weakened. Therefore, its influence is gradually decreasing. This is the final bulletin in this regard.
