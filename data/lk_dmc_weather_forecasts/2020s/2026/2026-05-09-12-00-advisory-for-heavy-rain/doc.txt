Bulletin No: 01 LRA/26/05/09/01 COLOR: Amber

Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 12.00 noon on 09 May 2026 valid for the period until 08.30 a.m. 10 May 2026

For Western, Sabaragamuwa, Central, Uva, North-central, Northern

provinces and Galle, Matara, Kurunegala districts.

PLEASE BE AWARE!

Due to the influence of the low-level atmospheric disturbance in the vicinity of Sri Lanka, Heavy falls above 100 mm are likely at some places in Western, Sabaragamuwa, Central, Uva, North-central and Northern provinces and in Galle, Matara and Kurunegala districts.

Therefore, general public is advised to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by heavy rain, strong winds and lightning during thundershowers.
