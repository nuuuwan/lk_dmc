Bulletin No: 01 LRA/26/05/12/01 COLOR: Amber

Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 03.30 p.m. on 12 May 2026 valid for the period until 03.30 p.m. 13 May 2026

For Western, Sabaragamuwa, North-western, North-central provinces and

Trincomalee, Galle and Matara districts

PLEASE BE PREPARED!

Due to the influence of the low-pressure area in the vicinity of Sri Lanka, Heavy falls above 100 mm with strong lightning are likely at some places in Western, Sabaragamuwa, North-western and North-central provinces and in Trincomalee, Galle and Matara districts.

Therefore, general public is advised to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by heavy rain, strong winds and lightning during thundershowers.
