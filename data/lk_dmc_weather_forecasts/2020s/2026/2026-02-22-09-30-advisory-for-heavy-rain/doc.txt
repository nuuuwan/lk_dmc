Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 08.30 a.m. on 22 February 2026 valid for the period until 08.30 a.m. 23 February 2026

For Central, Uva, Sabaragamuwa, Eastern and North-central provinces and in

Galle and Matara districts

PLEASE BE AWARE!

Due to the influence of the low level atmospheric disturbance in the vicinity of Sri Lanka, Heavy falls above 100 mm are likely at some places in Central, Uva, Sabaragamuwa, Eastern and North-central provinces and in Galle and Matara districts.

Therefore, general public is advised to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by heavy rain, strong winds and lightning during thundershowers.
