Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 12.00 noon on 10 May 2026 valid for the period until 08.30 a.m. 11 May 2026

For Western, Sabaragamuwa, Central, Southern, Uva, North-western,

Northern provinces and Anuradhapura district.

PLEASE BE AWARE!

Due to the influence of the low-level atmospheric disturbance in the vicinity of Sri Lanka, Heavy falls above 100 mm are likely at some places in Western, Sabaragamuwa, Central, Southern, Uva, North-western, and Northern provinces and in Anuradhapura district.

Therefore, general public is advised to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by heavy rain, strong winds and lightning during thundershowers.
