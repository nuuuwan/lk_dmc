Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 01.00 p.m. on 18 February 2026 valid for the period until 01.00 p.m. 19 February 2026

For Uva province and Polonnaruwa, Batticaloa, Ampara, Matale and Nuwara-

Eliya districts

PLEASE BE AWARE!

Heavy falls above 100 mm are likely at some places in Uva province and in Polonnaruwa, Batticaloa, Ampara, Matale and Nuwara-Eliya districts.

Therefore, general public is advised to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by heavy rain, strong winds and lightning during thundershowers.
