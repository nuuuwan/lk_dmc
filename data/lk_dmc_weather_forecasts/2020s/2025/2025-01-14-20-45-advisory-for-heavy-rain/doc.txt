නැකෙනහිර, ඌව, උතුරු සහ උතුරුමැද පළොත් සහ හම්බනකතොට දිසරික් ය සඳහො සැලකිලිමත් වනන!

සක්‍රීය ඊසොන දිෙ කමෝසම් තත්ත්වය කහරතුකවන,දිවයිකන දැනට පවතින වැසි තත්ත්වය ඉදිරි පැය 24 තුල තවදුරටත් බලොකපොකරොත්තු කේ.

Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 08.30 p.m. 14 January 2025, valid until 08.30 p.m. 15 January 2025

For Eastern, Uva, Northern, and North-Central provinces and Hambantota

district.

PLEASE BE AWARE!

Due to the active Northeast Monsoon conditions, prevailing showery condition in the island is likely to continue further during the next 24 hours.

Heavy showers about 150 mm can be expected at some places in Eastern, Uva, Northern, and North-Central provinces and in Hambantota district. Fairly heavy showers about 75mm are likely at some places elsewhere.

General public is kindly requested to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by temporary localized strong winds and lightning during thundershowers.
