Bulletin No: 26 TCW/25/11/30/03 COLOR: Red

Warning for Cyclonic storm “Ditwah” in the vicinity of Sri Lanka Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 9.30 a.m. 30th November 2025 for the period until 9.30 p.m. 30th November 2025

For the sea areas around the island

PLEASE BE AWARE! The Cyclonic Storm “Ditwah” [Pronunciation: Ditwah] is centered near latitude 11.1°N and longitude 80.6°E, about 170 km North-northeast of Jaffna. It is very likely to move northwards. (Cyclone track details attached.)

Page 2 of 3

Figure- Cyclone Track of “Ditwah”. Source: RSMC

For Sea Area:

Due to the influence of the above system, very strong winds about (50-60) kmph and gusting up to 70 kmph at times can be expected over the sea areas off the coast extending from Trincomalee to Puttlam via Kankasanthurai and Mannar and those sea areas can be very rough at times. Winds will be (30-40) kmph in the other sea areas. Fairly strong winds about 50 kmph at times can be expected in the sea areas off the coasts extending from Puttalam to Colombo via Negombo and from Trincomalee to Hambantota via Pottuvil. These sea areas will be fairly rough at times. The other sea areas around the island will be moderate.
