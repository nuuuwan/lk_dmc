Hambantota PLEASE BE AWARE! The sea areas extending from Galle to Pottuvil via Matara and Hambantota will be rough or very rough with strong gusty winds up to (55-65) kmph.

The naval and fishing communities engaged in fishing and naval activities in the sea areas extending from Galle to Puttalam via Colombo sea areas are requested to be vigilant in this regard.
