Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 04.00 p.m. on 16 December 2025, Valid from 08.30 a.m. 17 December 2025 to 8.30 a.m. 18 December 2025.

For Eastern, Central, Uva Provinces and Polonnaruwa District. PLEASE BE AWARE! Under the influence of the Easterly wave, the prevailing showery conditions over the Northern and Eastern parts of the island are expected to continue further during the next few days.

Heavy falls above 100 mm are likely at some places in Eastern, Central and Uva Provinces and in Polonnaruwa District.

Therefore, general public is advised to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by heavy rain, strong winds and strong lightning during thundershowers.
