Bulletin No: 03 LWA/25/10/28/01 COLOR: Amber

Advisory for Strong winds Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 11.00 a.m. 28 October 2025, valid until 11.00 a.m. 29 October 2025

For Western, Sabaragamuwa, Central, Southern, Northern, North-central and

North-western provinces, Trincomalee and Batticaloa districts.

PLEASE BE AWARE!

Strong winds of about (50-60) kmph can be expected at times over Western, Sabaragamuwa, Central, Southern, Northern, North-central and North-western provinces and in Trincomalee and Batticaloa districts due to the cyclonic storm “Montha” to the northeast of Sri Lanka in Bay of Bengal.
