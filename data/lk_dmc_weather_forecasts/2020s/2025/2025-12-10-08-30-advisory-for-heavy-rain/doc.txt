Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 08.30 a.m. on 10 December 2025, valid for the period until 08.30 a.m. on 11 December 2025.

For Northern, North-Central provinces, and Batticaloa and Trincomalee

Districts PLEASE BE AWARE! The Northeast monsoon conditions are established over the island.

Showers or thundershowers will occur at times in Northern, North-Central, Eastern, Central, Southern and Uva provinces.

Heavy showers above 100 mm are likely at some places in Northern, North-Central provinces and Batticaloa and Trincomalee Districts.

Therefore, general public is advised to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by heavy rain, strong winds and strong lightning during thundershowers.
