Advisory for Heavy Rain Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 01.00 p.m. 13 May 2024, valid until 11.30 p.m. 13 May 2024

For Central, Sabaragamuwa, Uva province and in Galle, Matara and

Kaluthara districts.

PLEASE BE AWARE! Due to a low-level atmospheric disturbance in the vicinity of Sri Lanka, showery condition is expected to be enhanced during the next few days. Heavy rainfall above 100 mm can be expected at some places in Central, Sabaragamuwa and Uva provinces and in Galle, Matara and Kaluthara districts.

General public is kindly requested to take adequate precautions to minimize damages caused by temporary localized strong winds and lightning during thundershowers.
