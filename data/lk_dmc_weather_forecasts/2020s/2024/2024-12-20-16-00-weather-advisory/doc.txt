Bulletin No: 06 WW/TC/24/12/20/01 COLOR: Amber

Weather Advisory Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 04.00 p.m. 20 December 2024 for the period until 04.00 p.m. 21 December 2024

For Multiday boats in the West-central Bay of Bengal Sea areas PLEASE BE AWARE! The low-pressure area over the southwest Bay of Bengal has now moved into the west-central Bay of Bengal. It is expected to gradually move north-northeastwards.

Advisories: For Sea Area

• Sea areas in the West-central Bay of Bengal will be rough at times as the wind speed can

be increased up to (50 -60) kmph at times. Showers or thundershowers are likely in these sea areas.

Action suggested for the Sea area:

• Naval and fishing communities, engaging activities over West-central Bay of Bengal are requested to be vigilant in this regard.
