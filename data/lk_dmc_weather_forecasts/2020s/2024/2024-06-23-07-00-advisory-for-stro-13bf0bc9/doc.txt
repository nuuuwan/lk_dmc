Bulletin No: 02 WW/O/24/06/23/01 COLOR: Amber Advisory for strong winds and rough seas. Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre At 07.00 a.m. 23 June 2024 for the period until 07.00 a.m. 24 June 2024

For the sea areas off the coasts extending from Trincomalee to Chilaw via

Kankasanthurai and Puttalam and from Hambantota to Pottuvil.

PLEASE BE AWARE

The sea areas off the coasts extending from Trincomalee to Chilaw via Kankasanthurai and Puttalam and from Hambantota to Pottuvil will be very rough at times with very strong gusty winds up to (60-65) kmph. The sea areas off the coasts extending from Chilaw to Hambantota via Colombo and Galle will be rough at times with strong gusty winds up to (50-55) kmph. Waves heights may increase (about 2.0–2.5 m) in the sea areas off the coast extending from Kalpitiya to Pottuvil via Colombo, Galle, and Hambantota (this is not for land area). Naval and fishing communities are requested to be attentive in this regard.
