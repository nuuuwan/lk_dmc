Bulletin No: 02 WW/O/23/11/28/01 COLOR: Amber Advisory for strong winds and rough seas.

Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 03.30 p.m. 28 November 2023, valid until 03.30 p.m. 29 November 2023

For Multi-day boats in the South-east Bay of Bengal and Andaman Sea areas. PLEASE BE AWARE! A low-pressure area has developed over south Andaman Sea and adjoining southeast Bay of Bengal. It is likely to move west-northwestwards and intensify into a depression over southeast Bay of Bengal around 30th November 2023 and it will further develop in to a cyclonic storm.
