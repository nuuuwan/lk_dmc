For Naval and Fisherman communities in the deep and shallow sea areas in the

southwest Bay of Bengal

PLEASE BE AWARE!

Naval and fishing communities are advised not to venture into the marked areas for the next 24 hours.

The Cyclonic storm “MICHAUNG” (PRONOUNCED AS “MIGJAUM”) over Southwest Bay of Bengal was located near latitude 15.5°N and longitude 80.3°E, at 1130hrs of 05 December and about 650 km north of Jaffna. The system moves nearly northward away from Sri Lanka towards Andra Pradesh coast of India. It would cross South Andhra Pradesh coasts during next hour.
