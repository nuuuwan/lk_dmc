Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 3.00 p.m. 15 November 2023, valid until 3.00 p.m. 16 November 2023

For Multi-day boats in the Central Bay of Bengal Sea areas.

PLEASE BE AWARE! The low-pressure area over Southeast Bay of Bengal adjoining Andaman and Nicobar Island is intensified into a depression and lay centered at 0830 hours of today, the 15th November over West-central Bay of Bengal near latitude 14.5°N and longitude 86.8°E. It is likely to move north-northwestwards and intensify into a deep depression over West-central Bay of Bengal Sea area in the morning of 16th November.

Wind speed will be (40-45) kmph and can increase up to (50-60) kmph in sea areas between (10N - 19N) and between (82E – 90E). Heavy showers or thundershowers and rough or very rough seas can also be expected in above sea areas.

Actions Required:

• Naval and fishing communities are advised not to venture into the sea areas bounded

by (10N - 19N) and between (82E – 90E) during the next 24 hour. • Those who are out at aforementioned sea regions are advised to return to coasts or

moved safer areas immediately. • Fishing and naval community are requested to be attentive to future forecasts issued

by the Department of Meteorology in this regard.
