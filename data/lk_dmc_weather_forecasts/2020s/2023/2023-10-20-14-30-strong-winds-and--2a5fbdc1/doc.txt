Bulletin No: 01 WW/O/23/10/20/01 COLOR: Amber Advisory for strong winds and rough seas.

Issued by the Natural Hazards Early Warning Centre Issued at 2.30 p.m. 20 October 2023, valid until 2.30 p.m. 21 October 2023

For Multi-day boats in the southwest and adjoining South-East Bay of Bengal

Sea areas.

PLEASE BE AWARE! A low-pressure area has formed over the southwest and adjoining southeast Bay of Bengal in the morning of today, the 20th October, 2023. It is likely to intensify further into a depression over west central Bay of Bengal around 23rd October.

Wind speed will be (40-45) kmph and can increase up to (50-55) kmph in sea areas between (10N - 14 N) and between (84E – 90E). Heavy showers or thundershowers and very rough or rough seas can also be expected in above sea areas.

Actions Required:

• Naval and fishing communities are advised not to venture into the sea areas bounded

by (10N - 14 N) and between (84E – 90E) during the next 24 hour. • Those who are out at aforementioned sea regions are advised to return to coasts or

moved safer areas immediately. • Fishing and naval community are requested to be attentive to future forecasts issued

by the Department of Meteorology in this regard.
