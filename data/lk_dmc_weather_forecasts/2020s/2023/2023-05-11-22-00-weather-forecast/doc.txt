For Multi-day boats in the Bay of Bengal Sea area. PLEASE BE AWARE! cyclonic storm “Mocha”over the Southwest Bay of Bengal has intensified into a severe cyclonic storm and lay centered near 12.2°N and 88.0°E at 17.30 hours today (11th May). It is very likely to move northwards and gradually intensify into a very severe cyclonic storm by 12th May morning over central Bay of Bengal. Thereafter, it is likely to recurve gradually, and move north-northeastwards with further intensification. The system likely to cross southeast Bangladesh and north Myanmar coasts around noon of 14th May.

Heavy showers or thundershowers; very rough, high at times seas can also be expected in above sea areas.
