Weather Forecasts for various places in Sri Lanka.

- [**4,677** documents](https://github.com/nuuuwan/lk_dmc/tree/data_lk_dmc_weather_forecasts/data/lk_dmc_weather_forecasts) (**5.8 GB**), from **2023-03-26** to **2026-04-08**, scraped from [https://www.dmc.gov.lk](https://www.dmc.gov.lk)

- In **JSON**, **PDF**, **TXT**, **Tables** (0%) & **🤗 Hugging Face**

- In **English**

- 🎓 Cite as **[arXiv:2510.04124](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.04124) [cs.CL]**
