Due to the heavy rainfall received since last night in the catchment areas of the Kumbukkan Oya Basin, there is a high flood situation within the next 12 hours in the low-lying areas adjacent to Kumbukkan Oya, covering the Nakkala, Kumbukkana and Maduruketiya Grama Niladhari Divisions in the Moneragala Divisional Secretariat Division, as well as the Okkampitiya and Gaminipura Grama Niladhari Divisions in the Buttala Divisional Secretariat Division. Accordingly, residents living in close proximity to the Kumbukkan Oya, together with drivers and passengers travelling through these areas, are kindly requested to remain extremely vigilant and to take all necessary safety measures to safeguard themselves from possible flood situations. Relevant disaster management authorities are requested to take immediate and appropriate actions in this regard.
