A considerable rainfall has been occurred in Upper and middle basin areas of Deduru Oya as of 12.00 noon today. Deduru Oya reservoir is now spilling at a rate of 5,038 cubic feet per second. By analyzing that situation and river water levels of hydrological stations in Deduru Oya, The warning mentioned in the flood warning message No. 01 issued for theDeduru Oya basin on 20th of October 2025 at 5.30 am, will be extended for the next 24 hours. It is requested to pay high attention in this regard by the residents in those area and vehicle drivers running through those areas. Disaster Management Authorities are requested to take adequate actions in this regards.
