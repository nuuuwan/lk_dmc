Due to heavy rainfall received in the upper catchment areas of the Mahaweli River during the past several hours, and considering the water level readings of river gauges maintained by the Irrigation Department along the Mahaweli River, there is a possibility of a major flood situation occurring within the next 24 hours in the low-lying areas adjacent to the Mahaweli River in the Yatinuwara and Gangawata Korale Divisional Secretariat Divisions of the Mahaweli River Basin. Furthermore, the public residing in close proximity to the Mahaweli River in these areas are kindly requested to remain highly vigilant and to take necessary measures to ensure their safety in the event of possible flooding. Relevant disaster management authorities are requested to take all necessary precautionary actions in this regard.
