A Due to the heavy rainfall currently being received in the catchment areas of the Maha Oya Basin and based on the analysis of water level readings of the river gauging stations maintained within the Maha Oya Basin by the Irrigation Department, it is hereby warned that a major flood situation may occur in the low-lying areas of the Maha Oya valley within the next 48 hours. During this period, there is a possibility of a severe flood situation in the low-lying areas belonging to the Divisional Secretariat Divisions of Alawwa, Divulapitiya, Mirigama, Pannala, Wennappuwa, Negombo, Katana, Narammala (Marandagolla Grama Niladhari Division) and Dankotuwa. The general public residing in these areas, as well as drivers and passengers travelling through these regions, are strongly advised to exercise extreme vigilance and take all necessary safety precautions.
