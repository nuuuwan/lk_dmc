A considerable amount of rainfall has occurred in some of the upper catchment areas of the Maha Oya Basin as of 11.00 p.m. today. Based on the analysis of the current rainfall situation and river water levels recorded at the Hydrological Stations in the Maha Oya Basin, there is a possibility of a considerable flood situation developing in the low-lying areas of the Maha Oya Valley belonging to the Alawwa, Diwulapitiya, Meerigama, Pannala, Wennappuwa, Negombo, Katana, and Dankotuwa Divisional Secretariat Divisions. It is requested to pay high attention in this regards by the residents in those area and vehicle drivers running through those areas Disaster Management Authorities are requested to take adequate precautions in this regards.
